🏦 Banking

Kraken becomes first crypto company to secure Fed master account access - CoinDesk dropped today — and it's a huge deal for Bitcoin.

For years, Bitcoin companies have struggled to get proper banking access in the US. The Federal Reserve master account means Kraken can now move money directly through the US banking system — the same way traditional banks do. No more intermediaries, no more correspondent banking headaches.

It's being called the "skinny account" for now, and the traditional banking lobby isn't happy about it. But this is historic — the first time a crypto company has gotten this kind of direct access to the Federal Reserve's core payments infrastructure.

What does this mean for living on Bitcoin? More infrastructure, more options, more ways to actually use your Bitcoin without converting back to fiat.
