Why Bitcoin Changes the Game

With traditional investments, you need to draw down your principal. With Bitcoin:

  • Appreciation potential — Your stack might grow, not shrink
  • No required withdrawals — Live off gains only
  • Partially spendable — Don't need to sell everything at once

Key Variables

  • Bitcoin price — Obviously affects your target
  • Your savings rate — More = faster FIRE
  • Time horizon — Longer = more compounding
  • Withdrawal rate — 3% = safer, 5% = more aggressive

The Power of Sats

Remember: you don't need to buy whole Bitcoin. You can stack satoshis (sats) — the smallest unit of Bitcoin (0.00000001 BTC).

£10/month = ~2,800 sats/day

Over 10 years at 50% growth = ~0.5 BTC
