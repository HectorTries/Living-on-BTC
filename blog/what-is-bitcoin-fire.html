What is Bitcoin FIRE?

FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) has become a popular movement in recent years. But what happens when you combine it with Bitcoin? You get Bitcoin FIRE — a revolutionary approach to achieving financial independence using the world's hardest money.

Understanding Bitcoin FIRE

Traditional FIRE involves saving 50-70% of your income and investing in stocks, bonds, or real estate. The goal is to accumulate enough assets that your passive income covers your expenses.

Bitcoin FIRE takes a different approach:

  • Bitcoin as the base asset — Instead of diversified index funds, you accumulate Bitcoin as your primary store of value
  • Scarcity-driven wealth — Bitcoin's fixed supply of 21 million coins means your holdings can't be diluted
  • Global accessibility — Anyone with a phone can save in Bitcoin, regardless of location
  • Self-custody — Your wealth can't be frozen or seized by banks or governments

Why Bitcoin for FIRE?

Bitcoin offers unique advantages for those pursuing financial independence:

  • Highest returns historically — Bitcoin has outperformed every other asset class over the past decade
  • 24/7 markets — No waiting for market open — trade anytime, anywhere
  • Fractional ownership — Start with as little as £1
  • No counterparty risk — You're not relying on banks or companies to honour their obligations

How to Calculate Your Bitcoin FIRE Number

Your Bitcoin FIRE number depends on several factors:

  • Your annual expenses
  • Expected Bitcoin appreciation
  • Years until retirement
  • Monthly savings capacity
