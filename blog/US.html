Strike's Bill Pay: Your Gateway to Bitcoin Living

Strike has revolutionized Bitcoin living in the US by enabling direct bill payments from your Bitcoin savings. Here's how to utilize this game-changing feature:

  1. Download Strike and complete KYC verification
  2. Connect your bills through the bill pay feature
  3. Set up automatic payments using Lightning Network
  4. Pay major utilities, credit cards, and loans directly with Bitcoin

Key Strike Features

  • Zero fees for bill payments
  • Instant Lightning Network transactions
  • Direct deposit integration
  • Automated payment scheduling

US-Specific Bitcoin Strategy

A simple, effective Bitcoin living strategy in the US:

  • Use regular credit cards for all day-to-day spending (earning points/cashback)
  • Pay off credit card bills monthly through Strike's bill pay feature
  • Set up direct deposit to Strike for automatic Bitcoin conversion
  • Keep your Bitcoin secured in cold storage until needed for payments
