Day 2 of the Kraken Card

March 3, 2026

Day 2 of the Kraken Card, and I'm still going strong! Today I treated myself to a cake — paid for entirely with Bitcoin. Around 27,000 sats for something sweet. It's still surreal how normal this is starting to feel.

The Little Wins

Living on the Bitcoin standard isn't just about the big moments. It's these small, everyday purchases that add up. Buying a cake with sats — something that would have required fiat a year ago — feels increasingly natural now.
