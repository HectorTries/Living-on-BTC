About the Bitcoin Subscription Calculator

This calculator helps you determine how much Bitcoin you need to set aside today to fund your subscriptions indefinitely. It backtests your strategy against historical Bitcoin prices to show whether your initial investment would have been sufficient.

How It Works

  1. Enter your monthly subscription cost
  2. Choose how many years of subscriptions you want to pre-fund
  3. The calculator determines the initial investment needed
  4. It then simulates this strategy using historical Bitcoin prices
  5. Shows whether your investment grows enough to sustain perpetual payments

Key Considerations

  • Bitcoin's price volatility affects sustainability
  • Exchange fees impact the amount of BTC needed for payments
  • Past performance doesn't guarantee future results
  • Consider funding multiple years initially for better sustainability
